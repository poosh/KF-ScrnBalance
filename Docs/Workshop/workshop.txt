[h1]Main Features[/h1]
[list]
[*] Featuring [url=https://steamcommunity.com/groups/ScrNBalance/discussions/9/4580716151550023598/]eXpert Challenge Mode (XCM)[/url]
[*] Featuring [url=https://steamcommunity.com/groups/ScrNBalance/discussions/2/4666237625653274273/]Social Isolation Mod[/url]
[*] New perks: [b]Gunslinger[/b] and [b]Combat Medic[/b].
[*] Balances weapons and perks, making each perk useful in the game.
[*] Perk levels up to 70 but no grinding required! Perk [i]bonus[/i] levels automatically adjust to the game difficulty and player count always to bring a balanced gameplay experience while allowing perk progression (making e-penis bigger).
[*] Perk bonuses for custom weapons (the ones you get from Workshop)
[*] New weapons: [b]Golden M79 Incendiary[/b], [b]Napalm Thrower[/b], [b]Laser Dual-MK23[/b], etc.
[*] Medic Grenade Launchers: [b]M79M Medic GL[/b], [b]M4-203M Medic Rifle[/b].
[*] [b]Tactical Reload[/b] for Assault Rifles, Sniper Rifles, SMG, and pistols.
[*] [b]Quick Melee Bash[/b]
[*] [b]Chainsaw[/b] consumes fuel and brings real massacre to the game.
[*] Changing gun skins "on-the-fly", e.g., switch from the regular to golden AK-47 on a key press.
[*] [b]30 game modes[/b] (some require [url=https://steamcommunity.com/groups/ScrNBalance/discussions/2/483368526570475472/]ScrN Brutal KF Bundle[/url])
[*] Enhanced server-side zed hitboxes (less wonky headshots)
[*] More than [b]400 achievements[/b].
[/list]
Most features are configurable (turn on only what you like).
